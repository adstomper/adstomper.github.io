Adblock Edge

Background:
In late 2011, one of the best adblocker in the market, Adblock Plus started to implement a controversial "feature" called "Acceptable Ads" which consisted of a Whitelist enabled by default to allow some websites that according to advertising agency "Eyeo gmbh" considered to be unobtrusive.
Their main justification to implement this "advertisement bypass" was a survey created two months before, and only available to certain  users.
First customers of the whitelist  were Wladimir Palant neighbours from Cologne, Germany, also a specially crafted global Whitelist was added "https://hg.adblockplus.org/adblockplus/rev/92898036c27e" to make a parking domain company happy.

Because of this i decided to create a fork called Adblock Lite, this one didn't fulfil my expectations, so after 10 months it's replacement was released "Adblock Edge"
Both extension completely removed "Acceptable Ads" feature and also removed or disabled by default other features like advertisement hit registering and the reporter (to report unblocked ads).
